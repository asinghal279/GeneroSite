Pop Bottle Rocket Event

In this activity, students use pop bottle rockets to use the concept of the path of projectiles by testing rocket launches at different angles. 2L pop bottle rockets are an excellent way to demonstrate the effects of forces on objects and provide many opportunities for observing, predicting, measuring and carrying out experiments while controlling for variables. Part II of this series of activities demonstrates how different launch angles can affect the distance a rocket travels. A basic understanding of rocket trajectories will help you increase how far your rocket flies (its range). The student with highest range will win the event.

  • Event Type: Offline
  • Proposed Venue: College Ground
Rules

Each participant should carry identity proof( college id). Students taking part in the events should register on the day of the fest at the registration committee. On spot entry is not obliged. The judges’ decision is final.
All the events will commence at the scheduled time; Punctuality will be appreciated.

SAVE THE HUMPTY DUMPTY

First time ever TECH ME introduces the new event " SAVE THE HUMPTY DUMPTY" In this event, show your creativity and take the EGG DROP CHALLENGE. In first round landing of parachute is observed and in second round the time of flight should be observed. After qualifying both the rounds the final round of event save your egg and take the hat of winner.

Rules
  • Drop the parachute
  • Time of flight of parachute
  • Egg drop challange
DISQUALIFICATION
  • Use of any kind of cushion like thermocol, foam, bubblewrap, foam etc..
  • Use of any metal
  • Free fall of parachute
  • If does not fit in the dimension box
  • Shooting or throwing of the parachute is strictly prohibited (especially in 2nd round)
