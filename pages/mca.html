Events

Presentation Competition

In this event the participants can stand alone or form up a team of 4 and will be given approx. 1.5 hr and a topic on the spot and they have to prepare their poster related to that topic on the given time and after that they will be called on the stage and they have to present their presentation in front of the judges. Genero'19 presents the presentation competition. So, get ready to create something incredible.

Rules
  • Prior online registration is required.
  • Participants need to submit the presentation on given topic.
  • The entries must be an original piece of work. Plagiarism of any sort is forbidden and can lead to immediate disqualification.
  • The presentation must be in English.
  • The time of presentation must not exceed 5 minute.
  • Every team must have their laptops with them.
  • Content should not be defaming or abusive towards any community, person, place, etc. However, individual opinions can be stated with decency and decorum.
  • Abusive, obscene or exorbitantly erotic content will be immediately disqualified.
  • Decision made by the judges will be final and binding.
Judgement

Attractiveness, design, layout, clarity, presentation, skill, relevance to the theme, creativity, imagination, originality and overall impact.

Time: 1.5-3 Hr
Event Type: Offline
Proposed Venue: Auditorium

Out of Waste

In this event the participants will be asked to use the waste items like banner, poster, bottles etc. that are no longer usable and will be thrown after the GENERO'19 event, to make something attractive from it.

Rules
  • Prior online registration is required.
  • Theme selection is open for everyone.
  • Each participant will be alloted maximum 2 hour overall for giving their idea an attractive shape.
  • Participant must not use any item that is the property of ABES without proper permission.
  • The organizers take the responsiblity to check on any sort of misconduct during the stay of the participants and would take necessary actions whenever required.
  • Every team must have their laptops with them.
  • The decision of judges will be final and binding.
Time: 2 Hr
Event Type: Offline
Proposed Venue: Aryabhatta Block 2nd floor MCA Dept Lobby

SPOIL THE TEE

This event is a painting competition in which participant will be provided with plane white T-shirt and they have to paint the Tee by their un-boundable imagination. This contest has solo/duet participation.

Rules
  • Each participant will have to paint their Tee in the given time limit.
  • Theme selection is open for everyone.
  • The Tees will be submitted and then judged by the judges.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and the top 3 Tees will be awarded
  • Only one Tee will be provided to a participant/team of 2.
  • It is mandatory for all the participants to submit the TEE.
Time: 2 Hr
Event Type: Offline
Proposed Venue: Aryabhatta Block 2nd floor MCA Dept Lobby
