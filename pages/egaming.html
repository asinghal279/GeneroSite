Choice of side

A knife round will be played to determine the side. This is forced by the server. The winner of this knife round will choose the side by typing .stay or . switch in the chat.

Server Crash

If the server crashes before three rounds have been completed (in a half), the server must be restarted and the half’s score will be reset to 0-0.

If the server crashes after the third round has been completed, the half must be restarted with start money 2000 and the score will be counted from the last completed round. The start money is set to 2000 and the team that lost the last round kills itself using “kill” in the console. This round does not count. The half continues with the following round. The start money has to be set to 800 again.

Player Drop

If a player drops the server will be paused at the end of the current round (see rule Game pausing).

The waiting time for a dropped player is 15 minutes. If the player or a substitute rejoins the server and both teams are ready, the game can be resumed. In case the dropped player does not reconnect within 15 minutes and there should be no substitute available, the team must continue playing with one less player.

Warmup

To start the match all contestants have to type ready in the chat.

Participants can choose to forfeit a match if they wish. Forfeiting will result in loss of the match and possible penalty points.

Restrictions

In general, all scripts are illegal except for buy, toggle and demo scripts. Violations of this rule will lead to a warning the first time. If in team matches more than 1 player played with wrong client settings and they have been warned before already, the match will be deleted. Here are some examples for illegal scripts:

Jumpthrow
  • Stop shoot scripts (Use or AWP scripts)
  • Center view scripts
  • Turn scripts (180° or similar)
  • No recoil scripts
  • Burst fire scripts
Rate changers (Lag scripts)
  • FPS scripts
  • Anti-flash scripts or binding (snd_* bindings)
  • Bunny hop scripts
  • Stop sound scripts
Illegal Software & Hardware Tools

Changing the game graphics or textures with the help of video card drivers or similar tools is illegal. Furthermore any kind of overlay that displays the system performance while playing (e.g. Nvidia SLI Display, Rivatuner Overlays) is illegal. Tools that only display the FPS are legal.

Content Of The Config Folder

In the config folder you are only allowed to have config files. Pictures, demos, screenshots and zipped files are forbidden. If an admin is hindered in his work because of too many unclear config files, penalty points (1-3) can be assigned. Protests and support tickets opened because of this rule are illegal and will be rejected.

FIFA 19

Rules

All the matches will be knockout. Every player will have a option to choose any of the teams present in FIFA 19. Consoles will be provided by the management team but for better experience, the player may bring his/her console/keyboard.

In case of draw till 90 minutes, game will proceed to extra time further resulting a penalty shootout to declare the result. Any misbehaviour caused by any player may led to disqualification.

Game Setting
  • Half length for initial stages will be 5 minutes.
  • Half length for semis and final will be 6 minutes.
  • All the remaining game settings will be set to default.
  • Game speed will be set according to the players facing each other.
  • Injuries will be set to 'OFF '.
  • Offsides, Bookings and Handball will be set to 'ON'.
Game Crash

In case of game crash the game will be restarted with the previous scoreline as refereed the official present for the particular game.

PUBG - Mobile

Rules

A team should also have a captain assigned who will be the point of contact with the officials. The captain needs to be part of the team roster for every game, and teams cannot change captains during the course of the tournament.

Each team member will have to be registered with their official gamer tags and once registered, no changes to the roster will be permitted. Furthermore, a Team’s name, once registered cannot be changed again without prior approval. The team names must be different and unique and must not include words or logos that infringe trademarks, are deemed offensive, toxic or harmful.

Squads

PUBG Mobile tournaments are usually played in teams of four. Each team is required to maintain four players in the team’s starting line up.There are no compulsion that the number of players in the team has to be four except for the fact if the team wants to play with one less player, but 3 is the required number to participate. Tournament officials reserves the right to disqualify teams if they are not meeting the required quorum of players.

Duo

Each team is required to maintain two players in the team’s starting line up. Tournament officials reserves the right to disqualify teams if they are not meeting the required quorum of players.

TEKKEN 3

Match Format
  • Registered teams will compete against each other to win out the battle royale and get that tasty chicken dinner.
  • But that's not all game will have scoring system and it is based on each team’s final kill count and in-game placement. 1 kill counts as 10 point. The final placement of teams are also taken into account. If there is a tie between two teams during any phase, the winner will be decided based on a TDM best of three.
Tournament Format
  • The tournament will be held on console or keyboard as preferred by the player.
  • Tournament will be a single elimination knock‐out tournament.
  • Each Match of the tournament will be played to a best of three Fights (First to reach two victories).
  • Each Fight will be set to a best of three Rounds. (First to reach two K.O.s).
  • Participants that lose their Match will be eliminated from the tournament and can play no further part, regardless of forfeiture or abstention from other participants.
  • The Final Matches of the tournament ‐ (Semi Finals and Finals) the Fights will be played to a best of five Rounds.
In Game Rules
  • Players will be given 3 minutes to calibrate their controllers according to their will.Once done, their settings will not be changed whatsoever.
  • Each round will be of 60 seconds.
  • You may choose the same character as many times as you like.
  • Watching match end replays is mandatory. Skipping might result in a penalty.
  • In case of a tie, a sudden death match will be played to determine the winner.
  • Sudden death match will consist of one round. Winner of the round will be declared as the Winner.
  • In case of hardware failure, the match officials will be responsible and the match will be restarted.
  • Players can bring their own hardware for failure of which, the officials will not be responsible.
  • Fighting with Mokujin is not allowed.
  • In any case, the decision of the officials will be final. No arguments will be entertained.
  • Use of provocative language and abusive taunting may result in disqualification.
  • Only the person registered for the match will be allowed to play – no replacements whatsoever.
