Business Conclave

“Good business leaders create a vision, articulate the vision, passionately own the vision, and relentlessly drive it to completion.”

The organisations which last for ages have been seen to have a common thread running in the form of great leaders taking the reins. Business Conclave introduces eminent personalities who have led organisations, faced their fair share of highs and lows to finally emerge as leaders. This year, our panel of speakers will inspire you with their journeys of conviction, determination and life lessons which will last a lifetime.

Marketing Mayhem

This event helps discover the marketer in you. Participants either in teams or as individuals are given some used/ refurbished/ recyclable products using which they can create an entirely different product thereby tapping the potential in the innovators. These final products have to be sold to students, teachers or anyone within the university campus. The winners of the event will be decided based upon the extent of creativity imparted in the making of the product, quality of the marketing strategy used, the number of items sold and finally, the amount of money that each team made. Marketing Mayhem will definitely test whether you are ingenious enough to overcome the various impediments that marketers and innovators brook around the globe

Beg Borrow Selfie

The teams will be given a list of 5 to 10 items. Within an hour, every team has to click selfies with as many items possible and report back to the concerned event head. Maximum five members per team are allowed. Teams can beg or borrow items from others.

Business Game

The teams will be given a list of 5 to ten items those items within one hour and take a selfi and they can beg and borrow and while collecting the item they have to take a selfie and report back to the concerned event head .There is no boundation for minimum members but a team can have a maximum of the 5 members.
In this game , there are two rounds :
ROUND 1 : Risk Management (Left and right thinkers)
