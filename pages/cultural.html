It is a solo dance event in which dancers from every college participate and showcase their talent. Depending on the dancer, styles can vary from western to classical to hip-hop and even to the typical Bollywood style. The event will be conducted in three rounds.

Round 1

Audition Round

Round 2

Nukkad Nattak

Nukkad Natak is a form of theatrical performance and presentation in outdoor public spaces. Nukkad Natak is used to convey a message to the crowd watching it. Nukkad Natak is considered to be the rawest form of acting, because one does not have a microphone or loudspeakers.

Rules
  • Time slot for Nukkad Natak: 24+2 minutes
  • Minimum number of participants: 10
  • Maximum number of participants: 22
  • Theme selection is open. All the teams can have a theme of their own choice.
  • Script must be original.
  • All props are allowed.
  • The production can be either in English or in Hindi or both.
  • Team will be judged on the basis of their voice, creativity and content
  • Exceeding time limit, Exceeding the number of participants will lead in deduction in final points.
  • A team will be disqualified if anyone found to make use of vulgar or abusive content.
  • The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
  • Event Type: Offline
  • Proposed Venue: Front Lawn

Vintage Wonders

An Ageless Adventure

Vintage Wonders is an amalgamation of style, sass and eternal beauty. This retro-themed fixture will witness an ageless experience where we give the participants an opportunity to dress like veteran actor and actresses. This event is meant to be a tribute to our rich Indian Bolly-Culture.

Rules
  • The participants are supposed to be dressed according to the Retro theme. Apart from this, a pair of western dress is mandatory (for the 3rd round).
  • No indisciplinary activity will be tolerated. Such a case will lead to the participant being disqualified.
  • The judges' decision would be final and binding.
Rounds
  • ROUND 1 BYGONE ECHOES
    This will be a screening round. All the participants will be allowed 20-30 seconds in which they are supposed to walk and pose (mimicking their concerned actor or actress).
  • ROUND 2 VINTAGE NOSTALGIA
    All the shortlisted participants are supposed to say a few lines about their character.
  • ROUND 3 ECHOES OF GLORY This round will be a questionnaire and brand promotions round. All the participants need to change in the dresses or accessories provided by the brand. One question will be asked by each participant.
