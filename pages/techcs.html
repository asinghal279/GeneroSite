Events

Geeks of the Galaxy

30 Hours event in which teams will come and create a product that solves any problem statement.Judges will be college faculties. We will circulate a set of problem statements before hand.

Pepper to your Tony : A Coders’ Date

30 Hours event in which teams will come and create a product that solves any problem statement.Judges will be college faculties. We will circulate a set of problem statements before hand.

Round 1
Elimination Round

We’ll be giving the participants a quizlet consisting of 10 MCQs. The participants with compatible scores will paired together, and the ones with the lowest scores will be eliminated.

Round 2
C For Chemistry

Set of incomplete questions shall be given to the participating pairs. The clues for the blank portions in the questions given will be hidden with in the college campus. The pairs need to search for them and once they know the complete questions, the first pair to code all the questions of their given set wins the competition.

Justice League

Tech Debate

There will be a debate on tech related topics. A judge faculty will choose the winner(s).

Design your hero

Web Designing

Participants will be given certain time slot to design front end of a website.
Participants will be made to pick a chit and make a webpage based on the character in the chit. We can also arrange workshops regarding same in Pre Genero events.

Are you Worthy?

Tech Quiz

This will be a Technical Audio-Visual Quiz. It will consist of multiple rounds ending with a Buzzer Round and Rapid Fire round.

In a Flash

Coding Event

This will be a classical Coding Competition for the coding geeks in or outside college. It will follow the pattern of ICPC Finals rules. We can also arrange workshops regarding same in Pre Genero events.
