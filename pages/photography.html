REAL LIFE SUPERHERO

You have to submit a picture clicked by you on the theme (YOUR LIFE'S SUPERHERO).
Your life's superhero entirely depends on your perspective. It may be a mother trying to save her child from intense sunlight by her dupatta, or it may be a construction worker , a farmer working in extreme weather conditions.
You have to justify the theme Real Life Superhero.

Rules
  • The picture should be an original shot by you.
  • Editing is allowed
  • Submit pictures in .jpeg or .jpg format only else the entry will be subjected to disqualification without notice.

NOTE: Submissions to be done online.

B-Roll Event Description

B-Roll is an incredible asset when it comes to creating an engaging video production. Having good b-rolls makes a video more powerful and engaging to watch and to connect with the story intended to portray.
B-Roll is footage that supplements A-Roll or primary footage.

Rules
  • You have to submit a video that is under 60 seconds of duration.
  • There is no particular theme to stick to.
  • You can use any type of camera to shoot the sequence be it a DSLR , mobile phone etc.
  • Any editing software can be used to edit the sequence.
  • Video data to editing should be your own work and must not be taken from somewhere else.
  • Judgement will be based on how creative you are.

THE GLITCHED FRAME

You have to submit a series of pictures clicked by you that depict a story.

Rules
  • The catch is that you are bound to using ten frames to tell us your story.
  • You are allowed to edit the pictures but manipulation in terms of adding or deleting elements through apps like PS is not allowed.
  • The pictures should be clicked on campus and delivered on the same day. Old pictures are not allowed.
