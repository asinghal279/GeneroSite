Comic Camp

Round 1: Doodle Dorks

“A lot of the time in animation is spent getting the story right – that’s something you can’t rush.” – Jennifer Yuh Nelson

Relatable characters, a compelling storyline and pure imagination, animation is the epitome of creativity. So unleash the animator within as you sketch, draw and design an interesting perspective, create new characters, and tell your own story through the graphic medium! Genero'19 presents the comic strip writing competition. So get ready to create something incredible.

Time limit: 2 Days
Event Type: Online
Rules
  • Participants need to submit a comic strip on any topic.
  • The entries must be an original piece of work. Plagiarism of any sort is forbidden and can lead to immediate disqualification.
  • The comic can be in English or Hindi or both.
  • Entries can be hand-drawn or drawn with the aid of relevant design and graphic software. The picture of hand-drawn comic should be uploaded and tagged with #DoodleDorks
  • The size of artwork must not exceed A4.
  • The entries can contain a maximum of 6 panels, each panel featuring a different scene.
  • Content should not be defaming or abusive towards any community, person, place, etc. However, individual opinions can be stated with decency and decorum.
  • Abusive, obscene or exorbitantly erotic content will be immediately disqualified.
  • Decision made by the judges will be final and binding.
Judgement

Attractiveness, design, layout, clarity, content, art work, relevance to the theme, creativity, imagination, originality and overall impact.

Round 2: The Publisher

The Publisher is an offine book cover designing event. Students can participate either in a team of two or individually.

Time limit: 60 Minute
Event Type: Offline
Proposed Venue: Seminar Hall, Raman Block
Event Discription

A genre and one word shall be allotted to each team/contestant and their task is to design a book cover in accordance with the provided genre and incorporate the given word in the title of the book.

Rules
  • The title of the designed book cover must be original and not copied from anywhere and must incorporate the provided word.
  • The design of the cover must comply with the provided genre.
  • Abusive, obscene or exorbitantly erotic content will be immediately disqualified.
  • The contestants shall be judged on the basis of:
    1. Creativity
    2. Presentation
    3. Content
  • Decision made by the judges will be final and binding.
Round 3: The Endgame

The last round of the event Comic camp is a three round comic based online quiz. If you think you have the knowledge and you trust your instinct then this is the place to be. Facts and figures revolving around the comics and the cinematic universe will spellbind you into this amazing race. After all, we are in the endgame now!

Time limit: 60 Minute
Event Type: Offline
Proposed Venue: Seminar Hall, Raman Block
Phase 1
  • Each team shall be asked one question. They can either answer it or pass it on to the next team.
  • For each correct answer +1 point shall be awarded and -1 shall be awarded for each wrong answer.
  • No points shall be awarded or deducted if the team passes the question onto the next team.
Phase 2
Double Trouble
  • Each team shall be asked one different question.
  • The participating team can either answer it or accept 0 points. If they choose to answer, they can raise the stakes. Raising the stakes would mean they shall get double points (+2) if they answer correctly and 1 point shall be deducted from the scores of the other teams. If they give a wrong answer they will have to accept -2 points and +1 points shall be awarded to all the other teams.
Phase 3
Rapid Fire
  • 10 questions shall be asked during the rapid fire round.
  • Each team shall be provided with a buzzer. The team that presses the buzzer first will be allowed to answer.
  • +2 points shall be awarded for each correct answer and -1 points shall be awarded for each incorrect answer.
  • If a contestant speaks up the answer without being permitted by the judges, one point shall be deducted from their team.
NOTE: The use of google or any other sources is strongly prohibited and will lead to immediate disqualification.

VALAR SLAMGOLIS: All Verses Must Serve

Slam poetry is a form of performance poetry that combines the elements of performance, writing, competition and audience participation.

Rules

  • Each participant is required to carry their college identity cards.
  • Theme selection is open for everyone.
  • Each participant will be allotted a maximum 7 minutes for their performance (including stage setup). Points will be deducted for exceeding the time limit.

  • The decision of judges will be final and binding.
Proposed Venue: SH, Raman Block

TAKE DE BAIT

Take de bait is a debate competition. It will be a highly structured activity that focuses on the construction of well-supported, well-reasoned arguments in support of, or in opposition to, a given idea. This contest has solo participation. Each speaker will present his/her speech in English/ Hindi and then answer 1-2 questions from the audience.

Criteria

The speaker will be judged on the following criteria, and will be awarded marks accordingly:

  • Interpretation of the Motion
  • Logical Presentation of Argument
  • Evidences and Examples
  • Audibility and Loudness Stance and Gestures
  • Address and Format
  • Elocution / Diction / Fluency
  • Rebuttal of interjection
Time Limit

Time limit: The speaker shall be given a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 3 minutes to present their speech. A warning bell will be rung after 2 minutes, and a final bell will be rung after the third minute. Exceeding the prescribed time, negative marks (-1 for every 30 seconds) will be awarded.

Proposed Venue: SH, Raman Block
Event Type: Online

QALAAM-E-KALAM

Qalaam-e-kalam is a creative writing competition with a twist. The participants will be shown a set of four pictures and provided with a few words. Their task will be to write a story connecting the four pictures. Bonus points for including the given words in the story.

Rules
  • The story must connect all the four pictures in a continuous format.
  • Bonus points will be provided for incorporating the given list of words.(+2 points per word). However it is not compulsory.
  • The story must be an original creation. Plagiarism of any sort is highly discouraged.
  • Judging parameters:
    1. Creativity
    2. Grammar
  • Negative marks will be awarded if the story exceeds the word limit.
Word limit: 200 words.
Time limit: 30 minutes
